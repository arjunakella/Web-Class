PARADISO: ABOUT US

The best taste in San Leandro!

Global foods and international wines share the menu at this stylish fine dining choice. Since opening it’s doors in 1996, Paradiso has become a culinary destination. Paradiso’s central location in San Leandro lends itself to be a prime meeting place for friends and family, both near and far. We celebrate life with you; whether it’s a quick weeknight bite, or a special occasion, we intend to provide a relaxed and high end dining experience. Paradiso has been consistently serving a wide variety of delicious dishes, with quality ingredients. We offer elite choices for our meats and seafood, constantly seeking the best and most sustainable products on the market. Our menu strives to accommodate those with dietary restrictions or special needs, offering gluten free and vegetarian options. The success enjoyed by Paradiso for over two decades reflects the vision, determination, and hard work of Owner Michael Wiesner, countless numbers of employees and the continued loyal patronage of all our guests.
